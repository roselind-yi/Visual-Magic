These test music files are innovative creations that combine traditional Chinese poetry with Doubao AI technology. Through AI, we have transformed classic ancient Chinese poems into modern music of different styles, infusing traditional culture with new vitality.

## Music Features

1. **Fusion of traditional culture and modern music**: Combining classical poetry with modern music styles to create a unique auditory experience
2. **Diverse music styles**: Including hip-hop, DJ, folk, R&B, punk, jazz, Chinese style and other styles
3. **Rich emotional expression**: Different music styles convey different emotional colors
4. **AI technology empowerment**: Using Doubao AI technology to transform the artistic conception of poetry into musical language

## Usage Method

1. In the music visualization interface, click the "Upload Audio File" button
2. Select any music file from the `examples_music` folder
3. Click the play button to enjoy the music and watch the real-time visualization effects
4. Try switching between different visualization styles to experience different visual effects

## Technical Notes

These music files are generated through AI technology, preserving the artistic conception and emotions of ancient poetry while incorporating modern music elements. They are not only test music but also innovative attempts to combine traditional culture with modern technology.

Through these music files, we hope to:
- Let more people understand the charm of traditional Chinese culture
- Explore the potential of AI in artistic creation
- Provide rich test materials for music visualization projects
